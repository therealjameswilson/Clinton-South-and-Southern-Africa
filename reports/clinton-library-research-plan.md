# Clinton Library Research Plan

This plan is scoped to the planned FRUS 1993-2000, Volume XXVII, South Africa; Southern Africa.

## Pull Order

| Batch | Target | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | South Africa collection finding aid | Core files on non-racial democracy, Mandela inauguration, de Klerk, Mbeki, Pik Botha, nonproliferation, and U.S. policy. |
| 1 | Angola collection finding aid | Core files on Lusaka Protocol, UNITA sanctions, Dos Santos, Savimbi, and UN peacekeeping. |
| 2 | Rwanda and Susan Rice/Rwanda collections | Boundary review for Great Lakes, Zaire, Burundi, humanitarian response, and UNAMIR. |
| 2 | FOIA 2006-0457-F Zimbabwe | Split packet into document-level records and identify policy significance. |
| 2 | Mozambique, landmines, ACRI, and Africa trip speechwriting | Identify whether public framing points to internal policy memoranda. |
| 3 | Arms trafficking and SADC-related leads | Verify regional-security relevance and terminology. |
| 3 | Public Papers, audio, and speechwriting files | Use for public context and annotation after policy records are selected. |

## Reading-Room Log Fields

- Collection and folder title
- Box, OA/ID, item ID, case number, or NAID
- Document title and date
- Participants or drafter
- Original classification and handling markings
- Page span and withheld material
- Release status and withdrawal-sheet information
- Candidate decision: include, context, exclude, boundary review, duplicate, or follow-up
